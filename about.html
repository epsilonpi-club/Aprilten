About us

The Journey of EpsilonPi

EpsilonPi, is the first forum in VBIT that majorly focuses on Machine Learning, Data Science, and Deep Learning.
With immense help from Dr. Jayant Kulkarni, Director of IQAC and management along with the strong support of our faculty coordinator, Mr. Praveen Kumar Padigela, Assistant Professor | CSE, EpsilonPi was launched officially on 10th April 2021.

To not stay merely ordinary or just stick to the basics, we want to encourage the idea of problem-solving and using the fundamentals to build projects.
Further, to step up a notch and gain wider recognition, we aim to provide an environment that enriches research and the ideology of writing and publishing research papers. We target to help everyone utilize what they learn in classrooms and integrate it with skills that are currently in trend in the world.We want to help and guide them for a better career as well as higher education opportunities.

In a nutshell, we provide a one-stop destination to enhance and grow while utilizing the best of resources. We nurture the idea of learning and encourage enthusiasm in doing so. EpsilonPi is dedicated to enriching the learning and research environment on the campus.
